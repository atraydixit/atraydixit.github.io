Oncko's three pillars

Combinatorially complete datasets

Gene interactions are critical to health and disease. There are 20 thousand genes, 200 million pairs of genes, and 1.2 trillion three-way combinations. No one has been able to experimentally test large amounts of this combinatorial space before...until now.

Predicting Clinical Trial Outcomes

We combine our datasets with expert knowledge, tissue specific expression, and genetic evidence for state of the art predictions (see one of our publications here).

Biomarkers

We use our data generation to segment patients based on mutation co-occurence and frequency.
