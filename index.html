Oncko's three pillars

Data at combinatorial scale

Gene interactions are critical to health and disease. There are 20 thousand genes, 200 million pairs of genes, and 1.2 trillion three-way combinations. It has not been experimentally possible to test large amounts of this combinatorial space...until now.

Predicting clinical outcomes

We combine our datasets with expert knowledge, tissue specific expression, and genetic evidence for state of the art predictions of which drugs will succeed (see one of our publications here).

Biomarkers

We segment patients based on our proprietary data mapping mutation co-occurence and frequency with drug susceptibility mechanisms. We aim to block resistance mechanisms for all disease subclones while minimizing toxicity.
